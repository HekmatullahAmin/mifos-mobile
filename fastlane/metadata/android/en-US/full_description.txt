Mifos Mobile is a mobile banking app allowing financial institutions using the Mifos X or Apache Fineract to provide an omni-channel banking experience to their clients.

Clients can download the mobile banking app and view their accounts and conduct transactions including:

* View Summary and Details of All Accounts
* View Account Statements
* Apply for new Accounts
* Transfers funds between savings
* Repay loans via savings accounts
* Transfers funds to other members of the financial institution
* Add new beneficiaries via QR code
*  3rd party transfers (coming soon)

Financial Institution can:
* Brand the app with logo for their financial institution
* Send event-based and campaign-style in-app notifications to clients

This app is only meant for demonstration purposes and before being used in any production environment should be hardened with proper security for the API and channel connections.
